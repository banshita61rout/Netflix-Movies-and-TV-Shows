## 📌 Overview
A data analysis project exploring Netflix content using **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** to uncover patterns in genres, countries, ratings, and growth trends.


## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze Movies vs TV Shows distribution  
- Identify top genres and trends  
- Study country-wise content production  
- Understand ratings and audience targeting  
- Analyze content growth over time

## 📊 Key Visualizations
- 📈 Content Growth Over Years
- 🎬 Movies vs TV Shows Distribution
- 🎭 Genre Analysis (Top & Bottom)
- 🌍 Country-wise Analysis
- ⭐ Ratings Analysis


## 🔍 Key Insights
- Movies dominate (~70% of content)
- Rapid growth after 2016, peak around 2019
- USA leads, followed by India & UK
- Drama, Comedy & International genres dominate
- Majority content is TV-MA & TV-14
- Some genres remain niche
- Conclusions - Netflix focuses on movies and mature content, with strong global expansion and a shift from rapid growth to quality-focused content strategy.
